About Us

From humble beginnings

In January 2020, our journey began within the confines of a modest 2,000 square foot leased space. Despite the limitations of our surroundings and tools, we honed our craftsman skills with unwavering dedication. It was within these constraints that we cultivated our passion for fine woodworking, driven by a commitment to excellence.

To flourishing expertise

Fast forward to today, and we’re thriving in an expansive 8,000 square foot facility equipped with top-notch machinery. Our growth isn’t just about space—it’s about deepening our craftsmanship and broadening our capabilities. At the core of our operation is a strong commitment to detail, where we use only the finest materials and joinery techniques.

Passion for your project

We’re proud to collaborate with homeowners, designers, contractors, and new home builders, understanding that each project is as unique as the people we serve. Our philosophy centers on enhancing every space with a seamless blend of design, functionality, and aesthetics.

With a dedicated focus on exceeding expectations, we aim to deliver high-quality workmanship on every project. Your vision drives us, and we’re devoted to bringing it to life with passion and precision.
